Mermaids Are Cool

In the month of may there is a special activity for artists it's called mermay. If you are familiar with inktober its like that, where you draw a mermaid each day in the month of may. If you have social media you can post your artwork with the hashtage #mermay and search for other artists mermay art. Time to start drawing!

  1. Mermay was created by Tom Bancroft
  2. The hashtage #MerMay will show you the community of other artists participating in MerMay
  3. Mermay is not as popular as inktober but as of May 2018 it is slowy rising in popularity
